Last updated: May 26, 2026

How to Delete Your Account

To request deletion of your PouchBuddy account and all associated data, please send an email to:

pouchbuddyapp@gmail.com

Include the following in your email:

  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • The email address associated with your PouchBuddy account

We will process your request within 30 days.

What Data Gets Deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your username and profile information
  • Your avatar photo
  • Your community posts

What Data May Be Retained

  • Price reports you submitted — these are anonymized and retained to maintain map data accuracy
