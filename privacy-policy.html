PouchBuddy Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

PouchBuddy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use PouchBuddy:

  • Account information: Email address and username when you create an account.
  • Price reports: Store names, locations, and pouch product prices you submit, including nicotine, caffeine, nootropic, and other pouch categories.
  • Location data: Your general location, used to show nearby prices on the map.
  • Usage data: App interactions collected via Firebase Analytics.
  • Crash data: Crash reports collected via Firebase Crashlytics (Android only).
  • Community posts: Content you submit in the community feed.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the PouchBuddy service.
  • To display price reports on the map for other users.
  • To maintain leaderboards and user profiles.
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs.
  • To serve advertisements via Google AdMob (free tier users).
  • To process Pro subscriptions via RevenueCat.

3. Information We Share

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the following service providers, who process it on our behalf:

  • Supabase — database and authentication.
  • Google Firebase — analytics and crash reporting.
  • Google AdMob — advertising for free tier users.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management.
  • Google Maps — map display and directions.

4. Age Restriction

PouchBuddy is intended for users 17 years of age or older, consistent with its App Store age rating. Where the app displays nicotine products, those products are intended only for adults of legal purchasing age (21 and older in the United States). We do not knowingly collect information from anyone below the applicable minimum age. If we become aware that a user is below the applicable age, we will terminate their account.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at pouchbuddyapp@gmail.com.

7. Product Categories & Nicotine Notice

PouchBuddy is a price comparison tool covering multiple pouch categories, including nicotine, caffeine, and nootropic products. Where the app displays nicotine products, please note that nicotine is an addictive chemical. Nicotine products are intended only for adults of legal purchasing age in their jurisdiction (21 and older in the United States).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at pouchbuddyapp@gmail.com.
