PouchBuddy Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

PouchBuddy ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use PouchBuddy:

  • Account information: Email address and username when you create an account
  • Price reports: Store names, locations, and nicotine pouch prices you submit
  • Location data: General location to show nearby prices on the map
  • Usage data: App interactions collected via Firebase Analytics
  • Crash data: Crash reports collected via Firebase Crashlytics
  • Community posts: Content you post in the community feed

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the PouchBuddy service
  • To display price reports on the map for other users
  • To maintain leaderboards and user profiles
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs
  • To serve advertisements via Google AdMob
  • To process Pro subscriptions via RevenueCat

3. Information We Share

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the following service providers:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Google Firebase — analytics and crash reporting
  • Google AdMob — advertising (free tier users)
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Google Maps — map display and directions

4. Age Restriction

PouchBuddy is intended for users 21 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 21. If we become aware that a user is under 21, we will terminate their account immediately.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email below.

7. Nicotine Warning

PouchBuddy is a price comparison tool for nicotine products. Nicotine is an addictive chemical. This app is strictly for adults 21 and older.
